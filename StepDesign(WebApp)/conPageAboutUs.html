About

Our Story

Step Dezign is a multidisciplinary firm, focused on interior design, lighting design and planning. We are a team of professional, energetic individual’s with talented designers and experienced managers available to guide our client’s through the flawless and timely execution of any residential design project. We manipulate forms and combinations from our everyday world to create an aesthetic, practical, and sustainable atmosphere that transforms a simple building or space into a place of happiness for you and your guests.

Our process

How our interior design process works
Step 1

Concept Creation

We identify the requirements of your space; dicuss style and color preferences, timeline, budget, and more

Step 2

Design Development

After a concept is established, We create a comprehensive design plan, based on information gathered during the consultation. This is also the phase where we work closely with an architect to develop your construction Documents, including Plumbing and Electrical plans.

Step 3

Planning and Procurement

When the design and selections of your home has been approved by you, the implementation process of the design begins. We get to work placing orders to insure merchandise is delivered on time.

Step 4

Construction

After all design has been solidified and materials have been ordered, your newly created space is installed down to the finest of the details.

Step 5

Project Turnover

After your new home is perfectly staged, we hold a follow-up meeting to ensure all is everything you hoped it would be and then walk you through the big reveal.
